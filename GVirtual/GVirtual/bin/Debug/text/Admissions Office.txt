Eisenhower House - Admissions Office

The Office of Admissions is located in the Eisenhower House, which is named after President Dwight D. Eisenhower. Following his two terms as president, Eisenhower, who served as a member of the College's Board of Trustees, wrote his memoirs here. 

To arrange an insider's tour of the Gettysburg campus, contact the admissions office at 800-431-0803 or 717-337-6100 or email: admiss@gettysburg.edu
